\subsection{Riconoscere una quadrica}

$x^2 - 6xy - 4xz - 8y + 2z = 0$

\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{llX}
	$
		A = \left[
		\arraycolsep=2.0pt\def\arraystretch{1.0}
		\begin{array}{@{}ccc@{}}
			1 & -3 & -2 \\
			-3 & 0 & 0 \\
			-2 & 0 & 0 \\
		\end{array}
		\right]
	$ &
	$
		B = \left[
		\arraycolsep=2.0pt\def\arraystretch{1.0}
		\begin{array}{@{}cccc@{}}
			1 & -3 & -2 & 0 \\
			-3 & 0 & 0 & -4 \\
			-2 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\
			0 & -4 & 1 & 0
		\end{array}
		\right]
	$ &
	$\det A = 0$ \newline
	$\det B = 121$ \newline
\end{tabularx}

$\det B > 0$ e $\det A = 0 \Rightarrow$ paraboloide iperbolico
